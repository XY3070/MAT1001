# FTC II  
$$\int_{a}^{b}  f(x) \, dx =F(b) - F(a)$$  
eg:  $\int_{0}^{\sqrt{ 3 }a} \frac{dx}{a^{2}+x^{2}} \,$  
$$\begin{align}
&=\frac{1}{a} \arctan \frac{x}{a} \Bigg|_{0}^{\sqrt{ 3 }a} \\
& =\frac{1}{a} \arctan \sqrt{ 3 } ,\quad x\in \left[  -\frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{2} \right]\\
 & =\frac{\pi}{3a}
\end{align}$$
